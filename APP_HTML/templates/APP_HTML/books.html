Books & Audiobooks

As a child I was not allowed to watch a lot of television, but books and audiobooks where encouraged. Reading is something I enjoy this day. Looking back I can contribute benefits to reading, such as: vocabulary, creativity, and experiencing new things just to name a few.

An audiobook provides the same benefits as books, although used differently. Arguably, audiobooks provide a greater benefit because: humans have been "listening" far longer then "reading", the speech speed can be adjusted, and listening can be done while doing other things.

The five genres I enjoy most are: fantasy, adventure, leadership, personal growth, social psychology (and business are tied for fifth).
