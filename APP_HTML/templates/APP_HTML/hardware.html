Computer Hardware

Mining Rig:

This was my favorite project to complete. I learned a LOT while completing this project. I learned about: hardware, software, back-end, security, and cryptocurrency for starters.

Shortly explained, I decided to mine ETH (Ethereum) for several reasons. That decision determined: which graphics card would work best, power needed, operational cost, and ROI. Next, this allowed me find the optimal: motherboard, power supply, CPU, and RAM. Finally, I needed to construct all the parts and set up everything online; allowing me to mine with millions of others.

List of the parts used:

  • MB : Biostar TB250-BTC
  • GPU: EVGA GeForce GTX 1060 6GB SSC (x6)
  • CPU: Intel 7th Gen Celeron
  • PSU: Corsair HX1000i
  • RAM: HyperX Fury Black 4GB
  • Explomos Mining Powered Riser (x6)
  • TP-Link Smart Plug (HS110)

Gaming PC:

This is the third computer I've built for myself. My method is simple, "build it to last and take care of it". I take this approach to most things (and life). I have a total of four hard drives and one m2 SSD. I really like the m2 drive and I learned a lot when researching why it's so much faster compared to a conventional HDD or SSD.

List of the parts used:

  • MSI Z270 Gaming Pro Carbon
  • Kabby Lake i7-7700K 4.20GHz
  • GeForce RTX 2060 Super
  • M.2 NVMe SSD Samsung 960 Pro 512GB
  • Hyper Fury 32GB Dual-Channel 2400MHz
